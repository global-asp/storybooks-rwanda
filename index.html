Storybooks Rwanda

Storybooks Rwanda is a free open educational resource that promotes literacy and language learning in homes, schools, and communities. It makes 40 stories from the African Storybook available with text and audio in Kinyarwanda, French, and the most widely spoken languages of Rwanda. Share and enjoy!
